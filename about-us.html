Local Ethiopia Tours With Yetenbi

"Yetenbi" means "Welcome" in Gurage, and that's exactly how we greet every traveler to our beautiful country. We specialize in authentic Ethiopia tours that connect you with ancient history, vibrant cultures, coffee traditions, dramatic highlands, and local hospitality.

Founded in Addis Ababa, we're proud to be a 100% Ethiopian-owned travel agency offering insider access beyond the usual tourist trails. From Addis Ababa city tours and layover tours to Lalibela, the Omo Valley, Simien Mountains, Danakil Depression, and custom private Ethiopia tours, we bring Ethiopia's wonders to life.

Visit Ethiopia with Yetenbi Ethiopia Tours, one of the best Ethiopian tour operators for tailor-made trips that leave a lasting impression. We arrange budget tours, mid-range safaris, luxury tours, historical and tribal journeys, wildlife photography safaris, bird watching, city excursions, Addis Ababa city tours, layover tours, day trips, Danakil Depression tours, Omo Valley tribes tours, Lalibela tours, Tigray churches and Axum tours.

Ethiopia Travel Essentials

Helpful Ethiopia travel planning information for visas, flights, safety, seasons, and routes. We refine every Ethiopia tour itinerary around your dates, interests, budget, and travel style.

The Country

Ethiopia is one of Africa's most diverse destinations for cultural tours, historical tours, coffee tours, trekking, wildlife, UNESCO heritage sites, the Danakil Depression, and living traditions.

Getting There

Addis Ababa Bole International Airport is the main gateway for Ethiopia tours. We arrange airport pickup, Addis Ababa layover tours, domestic flight guidance, and private transport.

Visa and Immigration

Many visitors can use Ethiopia's eVisa system, while requirements vary by nationality and purpose. Ask our Ethiopia travel agency for itinerary documents when planning private tours.

Best Time to Travel

October to May is popular for Ethiopia tours, clear mountain views, hiking trips, festivals, coffee harvests, and cultural routes, though some private itineraries work year-round.

Health and Safety

We plan Ethiopia tours with local conditions in mind and use experienced guides and drivers. Travelers should confirm medical advice, altitude planning, and travel insurance.
