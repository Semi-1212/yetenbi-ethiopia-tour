Addis Ababa layover tour & Bole airport stopover

Transiting through Addis Ababa Bole International Airport? Book an Addis Ababa layover tour or airport stopover tour with hotel or Bole airport pickup and fit Entoto Hills, the National Museum of Ethiopia (Lucy), Merkato, and a traditional Ethiopian coffee ceremony into 3–8 hours—ideal as a half-day Addis Ababa city tour or full-day layover before your next flight.

We specialize in private layover tours and small-group Addis Ababa transit tours for travelers on a long connection or short Ethiopia stopover. English-speaking guides, comfortable vehicles, and flexible routing around your flight schedule and luggage help you see the capital without stress.

Detailed Itinerary

Addis Ababa layover day itinerary

Sample full-day timing from hotel or airport pickup through Entoto, museums, lunch, Merkato, and coffee ceremony—your guide adjusts for half-day options, traffic, and flight connections.

  1. 7:00 AM
    Hotel Pickup / Airport Pickup: Meet your professional guide and driver at your hotel or airport. Brief introduction and overview of the day's itinerary.
  2. 7:30 AM
    Drive to Entoto Hills: Enjoy a scenic drive to Entoto Hills, the highest point in Addis Ababa. Take in breathtaking panoramic views of the entire city and surrounding mountains.
  3. 9:00 AM
    Ethnological Museum: Visit the Ethnological Museum, housed in the former palace of Emperor Haile Selassie. Explore exhibits showcasing Ethiopia's diverse cultures, traditions, and artifacts.
  4. 11:00 AM
    National Museum of Ethiopia: Discover the National Museum, home to "Lucy" - the 3.2-million-year-old hominid fossil. Explore Ethiopia's rich archaeological heritage, royal artifacts, and ancient treasures.
  5. 1:00 PM
    Lunch Break: Enjoy a traditional Ethiopian lunch at a local restaurant. Experience authentic cuisine and learn about Ethiopian dining customs.
  6. 2:30 PM
    St. George Cathedral: Visit St. George Cathedral, known for its distinctive octagonal form. This historic church is a significant religious and cultural landmark in Addis Ababa.
  7. 4:00 PM
    Merkato Market: Explore Merkato, one of Africa's largest open-air markets. Immerse yourself in local culture, shop for traditional crafts, spices, and experience the vibrant atmosphere.
  8. 5:00 PM
    Traditional Coffee Ceremony: End your day with an authentic Ethiopian coffee ceremony. Experience the traditional way coffee is prepared and served, a cultural ritual that's central to Ethiopian hospitality.
  9. 5:30 PM
    Return to Hotel / Airport: Conclude the tour and return to your hotel or airport. Your guide will ensure you have all the information you need for the rest of your stay.
