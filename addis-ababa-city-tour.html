Addis Ababa City Tour Highlights and Local Experiences

Our Addis Ababa city tour is designed for travelers who want culture, history, food, and local lifestyle in one smooth itinerary. This guided Addis Ababa sightseeing experience includes major landmarks like the National Museum, Entoto viewpoints, Merkato market, and cultural stops with a professional local guide.

Choose a full-day city tour, half-day city tour, food tour, shopping tour, or layover tour based on your time, budget, and travel style. Each route is planned to help visitors experience the best places to visit in Addis Ababa efficiently.
