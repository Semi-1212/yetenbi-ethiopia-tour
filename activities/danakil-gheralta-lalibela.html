8-Day Danakil, Gheralta and Lalibela Itinerary

This Ethiopia adventure tour combines Danakil Depression, Gheralta rock churches, Axum heritage and Lalibela landmarks in one smooth route.

  1. Day 1
    Addis to Semera, Afdera and Ahmed Ela Camp: Fly to Semera, drive via Afdera salt lake, and continue to Ahmed Ela camp in Afar.
  2. Day 2
    Dallol, Salt Caravans and Erta Ale: Visit Ragad salt fields, see camel caravans, then hike Erta Ale for the lava lake at sunset.
  3. Day 3
    Erta Ale Sunrise to Hawzen: Watch sunrise at the crater and drive toward Hawzen in the Gheralta area.
  4. Day 4
    Abuna Yemata Guh and Axum: Climb to Abuna Yemata Guh and continue to Axum with optional Yeha area visits.
  5. Day 5
    Axum Highlights and Addis Flight: Explore major Axum sites and fly back to Addis Ababa in the afternoon.
  6. Day 6
    Addis to Lalibela Northern Churches: Fly to Lalibela and visit the northern church cluster.
  7. Day 7
    Lalibela Highlands Excursion: Visit Asheten Maryam and Naakuto Laab with optional village and cultural activities.
  8. Day 8
    Lalibela Southern Cluster to Addis: Tour southern rock-hewn churches and return to Addis Ababa by flight.
