Trip Overview

Inspired by the 8-day adventure listed on yetenbi Ethiopia Tours—this circuit pairs the raw power of Danakil’s lava fields with the vertical rock churches of Gheralta and the spiritual masterpieces of Lalibela. Ideal for explorers who want both expedition grit and heritage depth.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

yetenbi Ethiopia Tours’ “8 Days Danakil, Gheralta and Lalibela Tours”.

1

Addis → Semera · Afdera · Ahmed Ela Camp

Fly to Semera, drive to the Afdera salt lake area, continue to Ahmed Ela camp beneath Afar stars.

2

Dallol & Salt Caravans · Erta Ale Sunset Hike

Visit Ragad salt fields, watch camel trains, 4x4 to Erta Ale base, sunset/night hike to the lava lake.

3

Erta Ale Sunrise · Drive to Hawzen (Gheralta)

Sunrise at the crater, descend, then drive via Mekelle toward Hawzen; optional Abreha Atsbeha stop.

4

Abuna Yemata Guh · Gheralta Cliff Churches · Axum

Climb to Abuna Yemata Guh; continue to Axum with Yeha Temple or nearby rock-sanctuary visits.

5

Axum Highlights · Fly to Addis

Stele Park, St. Mary of Zion compounds, Queen of Sheba ruins, Ezana stelae; afternoon flight to Addis.

6

Addis → Lalibela · Northern Cluster

Fly to Lalibela, explore Bet Medhane Alem, Bet Maryam, Bet Golgotha, and neighboring churches.

7

Lalibela Highlands · Asheten Maryam & Na’akuto La’ab

Monastic visits and optional village walk or cooking demo in the highlands.

8

Southern Cluster · Fly Lalibela → Addis

Bete Gabriel-Rufael, Bete Emmanuel, Bete Abba Libanos; afternoon/evening flight back to Addis.
