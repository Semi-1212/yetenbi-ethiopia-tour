Bird Watching Ethiopia & Ethiopia Birding Tours — Endemics & Classic Routes

With 800+ species and rare Ethiopian endemic birds, Ethiopia is a top Africa birding destination. Our Ethiopia birding tours target Rift Valley lakes, acacia savanna, highland juniper, and afro-alpine edge—ideal for serious listers and photographers booking a bird watching tour in Ethiopia.

Whether you want a focused 4-day Ethiopia birds tour, a week on the Great Rift, or an 11-day Ethiopia birding itinerary combining highlands and lowlands, we tailor pacing, hides, and dawn starts. Add a coffee plantation day for a memorable Ethiopia combined tour (culture + birds).
