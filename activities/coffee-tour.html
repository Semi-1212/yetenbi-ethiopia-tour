Frequently asked questions about Ethiopia coffee tours

What is included in an Ethiopia coffee tour?

Coffee tour inclusions depend on the route. Typical experiences include coffee farm or plantation visits, traditional Ethiopian coffee ceremony, tasting or cupping sessions, local guides, and transport. Multi-day tours can include accommodation and regional flights or drives.

Can I visit Yirgacheffe, Sidamo, Harrar, or Kaffa coffee regions?

Yes. We can arrange private Ethiopia coffee tours to major coffee regions such as Yirgacheffe, Sidamo, Harrar or Harar, Jimma, Bonga, Kaffa, and Bebeka depending on your dates and preferred coffee focus.

Is there a short Addis Ababa coffee ceremony option?

Yes. Travelers with limited time can book a half-day or city-based Addis Ababa coffee ceremony and tasting experience. It can also be added to city tours, layover tours, or longer cultural itineraries.
