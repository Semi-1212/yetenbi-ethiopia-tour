Tour Overview

Experience the best of Addis Ababa on a full/half-day layover tour. Visit the National Museum of Ethiopia and see the famous Lucy fossil. Explore the historic Holy Trinity Cathedral and vibrant Merkato Market. Enjoy panoramic city views from the scenic Entoto Hills. Discover the rich history, culture, and traditions of Ethiopia’s capital. Perfect for travelers with limited time book your Addis Ababa layover tour today!

Detailed Itinerary

7:00 AM

Hotel Pickup / Airport Pickup

Meet your professional guide and driver at your hotel or airport. Brief introduction and overview of the day's itinerary.

7:30 AM

Drive to Entoto Hills

Enjoy a scenic drive to Entoto Hills, the highest point in Addis Ababa. Take in breathtaking panoramic views of the entire city and surrounding mountains.

9:00 AM

Ethnological Museum

Visit the Ethnological Museum, housed in the former palace of Emperor Haile Selassie. Explore exhibits showcasing Ethiopia's diverse cultures, traditions, and artifacts.

11:00 AM

National Museum of Ethiopia

Discover the National Museum, home to "Lucy" - the 3.2-million-year-old hominid fossil. Explore Ethiopia's rich archaeological heritage, royal artifacts, and ancient treasures.

1:00 PM

Lunch Break

Enjoy a traditional Ethiopian lunch at a local restaurant. Experience authentic cuisine and learn about Ethiopian dining customs.

2:30 PM

St. George Cathedral

Visit St. George Cathedral, known for its distinctive octagonal form. This historic church is a significant religious and cultural landmark in Addis Ababa.

4:00 PM

Merkato Market

Explore Merkato, one of Africa's largest open-air markets. Immerse yourself in local culture, shop for traditional crafts, spices, and experience the vibrant atmosphere.

5:00 PM

Traditional Coffee Ceremony

End your day with an authentic Ethiopian coffee ceremony. Experience the traditional way coffee is prepared and served, a cultural ritual that's central to Ethiopian hospitality.

5:30 PM

Return to Hotel / Airport

Conclude the tour and return to your hotel or airport. Your guide will ensure you have all the information you need for the rest of your stay.
